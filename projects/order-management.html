About this project

A mobile application designed to simplify customer order management for small and medium-sized businesses. The app addresses the difficulty of management in SMBs by providing a convenient mobile solution.

Built with React Native for cross-platform compatibility, this app runs seamlessly on both Android and iOS devices.

Key Features

  • Customer order creation and tracking
  • Product catalog management
  • Invoice generation and export
  • Customer database with contact history
  • Sales reports and analytics
  • Offline mode with data synchronization
