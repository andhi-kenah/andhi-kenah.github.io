About this project

A comprehensive SaaS platform for generating social media content using artificial intelligence. The platform transforms simple prompts into ready-to-publish content in less than a minute.

Built during my internship at Etech Consulting, this project showcases full-stack development with modern technologies and AI integration.

Key Features

  • AI-powered content generation from prompts
  • User authentication and role management
  • Analytics dashboard for tracking content performance
  • Multi-platform content formatting (Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram)
  • Content scheduling and automation
  • Team collaboration features
